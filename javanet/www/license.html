About the package licensing.

 This package is licensed under the GNU LGPL, the technical legalese can be found here. I will try to summarize it. The license grants you the right to use this code, in your applications, without any payment of royalties to the copyright holder, he being me. Further, in a feature not present in the regular GPL: you can use this library in a proprietary application, without having to make the resulting code free.

Some people will argue that this makes the license "less free" than the GPL, and perhaps it is. However, this framework has its greatest chance to influence application development if it is a resource for all developers; not just the free ones. Just as with its objects, this framework lets all of its developers work together too; whether they have the ability to make their code free or not.

Don't misunderstand me, I love the GPL. I think it is the perfect license for free applications. Final point: I would most apprecitate if you would include a link back to this site with your applications. After all, together we can build a better Net. But to allow you ultimate in freedom; I'll even leave that up to you.

A free library, so free, it can be used for free, in free and non-free applications, freely.
