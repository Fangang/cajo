About the project licensing.

 This framework is licensed under the GNU LGPL, the technical legalese can be found here.

Let me summarise the two most important parts:

  • The license grants you the right to use this code in your applications without payment of any royalties to the copyright holder; he being me.

  • In a feature not present in the regular GPL; you are free to use this library in proprietary applications, without having to make the resulting code free.

Many people will argue that this second point makes the license "less free" than the GPL; in fact I also believe it does. However, this framework has its greatest opportunity to influence application development if it is a resource for all developers, not just the free ones. Just as with its objects; this framework also allows its developers work together, whether they have the option to make their code free or not. However, I completely endorse and admire the GPL: I think it is the very best license for free applications.

A free library, so free, it can be used for free, in free and non-free applications, freely.
