  • Eric S. Raymond, and Rob Landley, with whom I had the pleasure to confer with at length, at Penguicon. For encouraging me to open my most precious and personal source code; you made the case guys. I did it! By the way, congratulations Eric on your very excellent new book!

  • Chris DiBona, your advice and encouragement meant a lot to me at the start of this adventure. Your book was also a pivotal inspiration.

  • Esmond Pitt (a.k.a.) ejp on the Sun Java RMI forum; for patiently answering my, and many others questions over the years, especially as they became more esoteric. Also for your outstanding book, on my very favorite subject no less!

  • Fellow free software developer Jean-Marie Dautelle, for his experienced feedback to this project. And also for his most generous contribution; an Ant script to build the project example! Jean-Marie heads an excellent open source project; providing classes and packages missing from core Java, for real-time, scientific, and safety-critical applications. I highly recommend a visit to learn more about: JADE. He also provided us with the example properties file, translated en français!
